Cookies Policy

We do everything to provide our customers with easy, useful, and reliable services, and placing small amounts of information called “cookies” on your computer may involve this.

Here are some types of cookies we may use on our Website:

  • Essential cookies: These cookies are necessary for the smooth functioning of the website, suggesting that you cannot disable these cookies. These cookies help us track your login status and remember your login information, language you use, font size, display preferences, and other actions and preferences.
  • Performance cookies: We may use these cookies because they help us collect information about your website usage, and that information enables us to enhance our website’s performance. This way, we’re able to maintain the seamless service delivery.
  • Targeting cookies: These cookies help us track your internet usage patterns, enabling us to send you relevant promotional materials.

It’s your right to disable or turn off cookies on your browser, but this move may prevent you from accessing or utilizing some of the most beneficial website features or pages. You can manage cookies by getting instructions available in your browser settings.
